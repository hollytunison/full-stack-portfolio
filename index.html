About

I'm Holly Tunison, a frontend developer with a UX background. I'm wrapping up a full-stack bootcamp this fall 2022 with CareerFoundry. I'm also a part-time Computer Science student at Colorado State University, concentrating in Human-Centered Computing.

My passion for web development began a few years ago while taking a web design class, during my senior year at the University of Colorado Denver. I fell in love with HTML and CSS. I was then able to land my first freelance web design role.

In the past few years, I have been working as a UX/UI designer and frontend web developer. My design, development, and communication background aids in communicate well with across teams.

I currently live outside Raleigh, NC with my partner Brennan, and our dog Obi. We like to camp and surf on along the Outer Banks. I'm in the process of developing a blog about camping and surfing in North Carolina, so check back soon for updates!
