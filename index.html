About

As a frontend web developer and UX designer, I bring a unique combination of technical and creative skills to the table. I have a strong background in both areas, allowing me to create visually appealing and highly functional websites and applications that prioritize user experience.

I have hands-on experience with various programming languages and frameworks, including JavaScript, HTML, CSS, React, and node.js. I also have a solid understanding of essential web development principles like responsive design and cross-browser compatibility.

In addition to my technical expertise, I have a keen eye for design and a deep understanding of user experience principles. I leverage this knowledge to design intuitive and user-friendly interfaces that facilitate seamless interactions and help users achieve their goals effortlessly.

To further enhance my skills, I'm excited to pursue a UX graduate program starting in August 2023. Currently based near Raleigh, NC, during my free time, I enjoy camping and surfing on the Outer Banks.
